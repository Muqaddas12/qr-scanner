Privacy Policy
QR & Barcode Scanner respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.
1. Information We Collect
We do not collect any personal data from users.
The app may require the following permissions:
Camera – used only to scan QR codes and barcodes.
Storage / Media Access (optional) – used only to scan QR codes from images saved on your device.
2. How We Use Information
Camera access is used only for scanning
No scanned data is stored on our servers
All processing happens locally on your device
3. Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.
The app does not use analytics or tracking tools.
4. Internet Usage
The app works offline
No data is sent to external servers
5. Children’s Privacy
This app is safe for all age groups and does not knowingly collect any data from children.
6. Security
We prioritize user safety. Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of data leakage.
7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy when required. Any changes will be reflected here.
